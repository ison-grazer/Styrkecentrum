IMPORTANT INFORMATION


THE GYM

Our facilities are freshly renovated and the locker rooms are equipped with showers and sauna. Bring a padlock for the locker. The gym has machines, free weights and class studios.

Our trainers are experienced and gladly answers questions and provides information about new exercises.


CLASSES

There are many different classes you can participate in. There is one class every day except for Thursdays. The classes start at 18:00 o’clock Friday, Saturday and Sunday. The remaining days the classes start at 20:00 o’clock. Questions about the classes as well as booking is done with Pia at the front desk.
