MACHINES

We have a great variety of machines available, for almost every possible muscle group. Every machine has a sign or a sticker on it containing instructions on how to use the equipment safely and how to adjust it properly. As per our guests wishes we also bought a new leg press!

In addition to the strength training machines we have a section of the gym dedicated to cardio. It is equipped with treadmills, cross trainers, stationary bikes and rowing machines.
