EVENTS

DECEMBER

With Christmas right around the corner we would like to invite out members to a half-day lecture on food and training at the city's local Hotel. Lunch and fika is included in the price. Members price is 150 kr and for the general public 240 kr.

NOVEMBER

During the month of November we host our annual Zumba-party. All members are wellcome and no prior experience is required. The evening is spent dancing and mingling to liven up the fall darkness.

OTOBER

Drop-in for cardiovascular tests will be available from the 1:st of October until the 8:th.

Did you know that you can buy profile items from Pia at the front desk? Members recives 20% off and during October month members receive a free gift.

SEPTEMBER

Our trainers are available for personal training for smaller groups as well as for one person. A one-time session costs 180 kr.

SNOW TURNS OUTDOOR-TRAINING INSIDE

It’s way too slippery outside at this time of year, the outdoor-classes moves indoors. The focus is still on cardio. We change our running classes to spinning and HIIT. Welcome!

FALL NEWS IN THE GYM

We have 4 ski machines visiting our gym during the winter months, don’t miss out on using them. There will be 3 opportunities for trainer-led classes on how to best use them. If you want to attend one of these introductionclasses please contact Pia at the front desk.
